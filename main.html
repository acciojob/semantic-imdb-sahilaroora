Movie Summary

*Inception* is a science fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. The story follows Dom Cobb, a professional thief who specializes in infiltrating the subconscious to steal secrets through dream-sharing technology. He is offered a chance to have his criminal history erased as payment for planting another person's idea into a target's subconscious.
